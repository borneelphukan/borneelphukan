## About Me

I am a Full-Stack Software Engineer based in Germany with over 3 years of experience designing scalable web applications, backend services, REST APIs, and cloud-native software systems.

I hold an M.Sc. in Web Engineering from Technische Universität Chemnitz, where my interests evolved toward the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Bioinformatics, and Decentralized Systems. Alongside professional software development, I actively explore research problems involving machine learning, knowledge representation, and distributed computing.

My long-term goal is to pursue a PhD focused on applying AI to either computational biology and precision medicine or decentralized, trustworthy web infrastructures.

I am also the co-founder and lead architect of Graminate, an AI-powered Agro-ERP platform being developed in collaboration with Assam Agricultural University.

## Research Interests

My research interests focus on applying Artificial Intelligence to complex scientific and distributed systems problems.

### AI for Precision Medicine & Bioinformatics

Exploring the application of genomic foundation models, transformer architectures, and protein structure prediction systems to improve variant interpretation in rare genetic diseases. Particular interests include genomic sequence modeling, explainable AI, and AI-assisted therapeutic design.

### Decentralized Neuro-Symbolic Systems

Investigating architectures that combine local language models, symbolic reasoning, knowledge graphs, and privacy-preserving computation. The goal is to develop trustworthy distributed AI systems capable of operating independently from centralized cloud infrastructures.
